Cloud Computing

I'm looking to grow the depth of my knowledge & experience relating to cloud computing & enterprise systems. I'd like to be able to add some exciting Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, or Google Cloud Platform (GCP) projects to this portfolio over the next 5 years.

Prior Cloud Experience

I took an intro to cloud computing class during my undergrad which allowed me to explore the Big 3 mentioned above. However, the projects completed for that class were magnitudes of scale lessor than that of which I'd expect in the industry. The class provided a comprehensive understanding of the history of cloud computing, the market(s) which it fills, and the concepts/theory behind how it works. The homeworks & projects gave me the opportunity to play around in some very small sandboxes.

Why Cloud Computing?

Cloud computing & cloud technologies open the door to endless possibilities. They allow for very small teams & even individual developers to build some truly incredible projects by offloading resources, storage, etc. Moreover, cloud solutions are future of software development & information technology. For the past few decades, there has been a mass migration of companies porting systems over to the cloud. In such a cloud-dependent society, I'd hardly be able to call myself a software developer without a thorough understanding & extensive portfolio of cloud-based experience.
